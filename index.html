Sourcing standard

Direct company boards first. Selected market feeds where they add reach.

The board is designed to prioritise direct ATS sources such as Greenhouse and Lever wherever they are available. That keeps role detail closer to the hiring company, improves description depth and reduces unnecessary duplication.

Selected market feeds remain useful for wider coverage, but they should support the board rather than define it. The stronger version of this product is source-diverse, current and selective.
